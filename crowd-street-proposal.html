Crowd Street Proposal by SH/FT

June 22, 2025

Project Name: Customer Journey Optimization & Automation

01 High-Level Project Description

This project is designed to elevate CrowdStreet's customer experience. We'll begin with an assessment of current email and journey practices, followed by a collaborative workshop and strategic mapping session. The culmination will be the implementation of an automated MVP journey within Salesforce Marketing Cloud (SFMC).

Our goal is to identify key areas for improvement and provide the necessary oversight to launch a critical customer journey, driving more effective engagement. We aim to leverage SFMC's capabilities to streamline and automate key customer touchpoints.

02 Project Deliverables

Here's a detailed list of the deliverables you'll receive throughout the project:

1 Phase 1: Email & Customer Experience Assessment +

  1. Documented summary of findings from email assessment
  2. Documented review of existing journey mapping

2 Phase 2: Customer Experience Workshop & Mapping +

  1. Documented existing customer experience
  2. Documented summary of findings from discovery calls and virtual workshop

3 Phase 3: MVP Journey Automation (Oversight Only) +

  1. Campaign brief for the chosen MVP journey (1 journey with up to 4 touchpoints).
  2. Messaging strategy, content strategy, and creative recommendations for the MVP journey.
  3. Blueprint document for the tech team to build the MVP journey in SFMC.
  4. Review of/Build and Assistance of data segmentation work.
  5. Build and Assistance of MVP journey testing and refinement.
  6. Build and Assistance of MVP journey deployment within SFMC.
  7. Build and Assistance of post-launch monitoring for two weeks.
