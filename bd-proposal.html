BD Marketo Instance Migration Proposal

A Strategic Transition to Regional Workspaces presented by Shift Paradigm | Revised Oct 23rd 2025

Project Objective

This proposal outlines the comprehensive plan to migrate BD's marketing operations from a single Edwards Marketo instance to a series of new, regionally-focused BD Workspaces. This strategic initiative includes the seamless movement of all relevant programs, forms, operational flows, and associated assets. We will align the new workspaces with BD's current operational processes and enable the APM team to ensure a smooth and efficient transition.

Considerations and Assumptions

Migration Path: The proposed approach assumes a direct instance-to-instance connection is possible, potentially via a sandbox environment. This is the most efficient method.

Contingency: If a direct connection is not permitted and a manual migration is required, the project scope, timeline, and cost will need to be re-evaluated. We will seek to confirm the migration path during Phase 1 before proceeding.

Assumes adoption of future global inquiry model; current model will not be implemented.

Assumes adoption of the future preference center and consent management redesign.

CRM Scope (Guidance Only): Within this revised proposal, we have included guidance only in SF CRM related to the lead and inquiry model and lead management. No build or integration work in the CRM is included in this scope.

Activity Data: Marketo activity migration may be limited to Program Membership based - dependant on available data (Source instance ID's).

Asset Migration: BD to manage and deliver all design asset migration - no requirement for design and development of email and landing page templates in destination instances.
