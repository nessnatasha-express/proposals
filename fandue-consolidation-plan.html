FanDuel Braze Workspace Consolidation

Interactive Project Plan

Project Overview

Core Objective:

To consolidate FanDuel's 7 active Braze workspaces into a single, new instance. This will streamline marketing operations, reduce costs, enable better cross-product promotion, and centralize customer engagement efforts.

Desired Business Outcomes:

  • Cost Savings: Reduce MarTech data usage costs (targeting up to 15% / $414K annually) and ongoing operational/maintenance expenses.
  • Increased Efficiency: Simplify campaign orchestration, development, testing, workspace maintenance, and user permission management.
  • Improved Performance: Faster product innovation cycles and increased revenue per user through enhanced cross-promotion capabilities.
  • Centralization: A unified platform for all marketing communications.

Overall Approach & Strategy

  • Collaborative Planning: Work closely with FanDuel stakeholders during discovery to align on priorities and requirements.
  • Phased Migration: Execute the migration in manageable stages, potentially prioritized by:
    • Alignment with FanDuel's seasonal promotional calendar and existing marketing/tech priorities.
    • Size and volume of assets.
    • Product line focus.
  • Clear Asset Definition: Utilize the FanDuel-provided audit and subsequent discovery sessions to clearly define all assets for migration.
  • Leverage Existing Infrastructure: The new Braze instance will be configured by FanDuel; this project focuses on the migration of assets into that pre-configured environment.
  • Rigorous Testing: Implement a comprehensive QA plan to ensure a seamless transition and mitigate risks.
  • Regular Communication: Maintain transparent and consistent communication on progress, risks, and issues.

Core Assumptions

  • FanDuel will provide an initial audit of existing workspaces and assets.
  • FanDuel will configure the core new Braze workspace/instance prior to the migration phase. This project team is responsible for migrating assets into it.
  • FanDuel stakeholders will be available for necessary discovery sessions (up to 3, max 60 mins each) and ongoing collaboration.
  • The scope of migration includes approximately:
    • Up to 300 Campaigns
    • Up to 700 Canvases
    • Up to 830 Content Blocks
    • Up to 20 Templates (general) & 20 Email Templates
    • Up to 10 Teams and 50 Tags to be created/mapped
    • Media library
  • A CDP Segment/Hightouch review and audit for consolidation is included.
  • Necessary access to existing Braze workspaces and the new target workspace will be provided.
