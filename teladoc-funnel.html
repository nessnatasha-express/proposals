Teladoc B2B2C Activation Funnel

From Partner to Patient

Teladoc's B2B2C Virtual Care Activation Journey

Employer to Employee Activation (B2B)

1. Notification

Employer announces Teladoc benefit via Open Enrollment materials and internal channels.

2. Identity (Targeted Outreach)

Teladoc sends personalized emails/SMS from 'HR' for high-priority enrollment.

Motivation: Convenience & Time-Savings

Payer to Member Activation (B2B)

1. Notification

Health Plan lists Teladoc as a covered service in Member portals and welcome kits.

2. Identity (Targeted Outreach)

Teladoc targets members with relevant health conditions to promote specific programs (e.g., Chronic Care).

Motivation: Cost Savings & Care Gap Closure

From Interest to Integrated Care (B2C)

3. Verification

~95% Conversion

Member uses ID/credentials to access app and undergoes instant eligibility check.

Channels & Micro-Messaging (Speed & Security):

  • Channels: SMS (High Priority), Email (Transactional).
  • Message: "Action Required: Complete your security check! Tap to instantly verify your eligibility and unlock your benefits."

4. Profiling

~80% Conversion

User creates secure profile and completes initial health screening questionnaire.

Channels & Micro-Messaging (Personalization & Value):

  • Channels: Push Notification, In-App Banner, Email (Nurture).
  • Message: "Welcome, [Name]! Finish your 5-min health assessment to get matched with your ideal Primary Care team."

5. Onboarding (Chronic Care)

~60% Conversion

Member receives and pairs connected device (e.g., BP cuff) for data synchronization.

Channels & Micro-Messaging (Action & Device Use):

  • Channels: SMS (Triggered), Push Notification, In-App Guide.
  • Message: "Your BP cuff has arrived! Text 'READY' to start activation steps and meet your Health Coach."

6. First Visit / Interaction

~85% Conversion

Member books and attends their first clinical session (PCP, Therapy, or Coaching).

Channels & Micro-Messaging (Logistics & Assurance):

  • Channels: SMS (Reminder), Push Notification (10-min prior), Calendar Invite.
  • Message: "Reminder: Your appointment with Dr. [Name] is in 10 minutes. Click here to join the virtual waiting room."

7. Sustained Engagement

~75% Retention

Ongoing care, follow-ups, integrated service use, driving client ROI.

Channels & Micro-Messaging (Adherence & Accountability):

  • Channels: In-App (Metrics), Push (Habit Nudge), Email (Progress Report), Paid Media (Retargeting unused benefits).
  • Message: "Check-in: You missed your weekly coaching session. Let's get back on track! [Link to reschedule]"
