Proposal: BD Marketo Instance Migration

BD Marketo Instance Migration Proposal

A Strategic Transition to Regional Workspaces presented by Shift Paradigm

Project Objective

This proposal outlines the comprehensive plan to migrate BD's marketing operations from a single Edwards Marketo instance to a series of new, regionally-focused BD Workspaces. This strategic initiative includes the seamless movement of all relevant programs, forms, operational flows, and associated assets. We will align the new workspaces with BD's current operational processes, including compliance, consent programs, and lead management protocols, ensuring a smooth and efficient transition.

Important Considerations

Migration Path: The proposed approach assumes a direct instance-to-instance connection is possible, potentially via a sandbox environment. This is the most efficient method.

Contingency: If a direct connection is not permitted and a manual migration is required, the project scope, timeline, and cost will need to be re-evaluated. We will seek to confirm the migration path during Phase 1 before proceeding.

Process Alignment: All work will be performed in alignment with BD's existing operational processes, including compliance, consent, and lead management programs, to ensure consistency and adherence to internal standards.

CRM Scope: Within this proposal we have included guidance only in SF CRM related to lead and inquiry model and lead management. Should you want to include any build or integration work, this would be scoped separately.

Activity Data: Marketo activity migration may be limited to Program Membership based - dependant on available data (Source instance ID's).

Asset Migration: BD to manage and deliver all design asset migration - no requirement for design and development of email and landing page templates in destination instances.
