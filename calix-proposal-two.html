Proposal for Calix

Scalable GTM Technology Roadmap & Use Case Plan presented by Shift Paradigm on September 26th, 2025

This proposal is a fast, focused strategic engagement designed to build a clear technology roadmap for Calix’s marketing, sales, and workforce systems. Our goal is to show you exactly what’s driving growth and what’s holding you back so you can invest wisely as you scale. We’ll map out which tools are helping and which aren’t, giving your new CMO a complete view of the landscape and a blueprint for smart decisions across Adobe, Salesforce, and other key platforms in the year ahead.

Objective

To support and guide Calix in defining its immediate and future technology needs. We will deliver a comprehensive Growth-Centered Technology Blueprint that provides a clear, budget-conscious, and phased plan for your go-to-market (GTM) technology stack, ensuring it serves as an accelerant for your strategic objectives.

Our Approach & Timeline

Our methodology is designed to be swift yet thorough, fitting within Q4 to prepare you for next year's planning. It consists of three key phases, focusing on delivering maximum value and clarity in a condensed timeframe.

Phase 1

Alignment

1-2 weeks
  • Stakeholder Alignment & Brief
  • Leadership working session to define goals
  • Survey skills, needs and gaps
  • Prioritization & alignment
  • Project brief that defines core outcomes
  • System Access

Phase 2

Unpack & Define

2-3 weeks
  • Workshop + Technical audits
  • 1-day workshop (complimentary)
  • Stakeholder interviews
  • High-Level Systems audits (up to 5 tools)
  • Perform gap analysis & maturity scoring
  • Identify and prioritize use cases

Phase 3

Technology Roadmap

3-4 weeks
  • Distill findings into a strategic roadmap
  • Multi-year tech roadmap
  • Recommended approaches for selected technologies across Adobe and Salesforce ecosystem and beyond if determined non-ecosystem tools are recommended.
  • Tech, data, and integration needs
  • Technical diagrams
  • Impact & Effort matrix with pricing
  • Final Readout & Next Steps
  • Executive summary and readout
