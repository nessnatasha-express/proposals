2. Bringing it to Life: Use Cases for Cornerstone

Holistic Customer View

Create a holistic view of the customer experience through cross-channel, real-time customer journey reporting.

B2B & B2C Brand Support

Enable both B2B and B2C brands and solutions to be managed through the new marketing engine with a Digital COE to support campaign and automation efficiencies.

Engage architects with technical specs and design inspiration. When they specify a Cornerstone product in a project plan, automatically alert the relevant commercial sales rep for high-touch follow-up.

Empower your channel partners with pre-built, co-brandable campaigns they can launch locally. Capture lead data centrally to measure channel effectiveness and ROI.

Identify a homeowner who purchased new windows (via Residential CRM data). Months later, automatically trigger a journey promoting complementary siding or doors, leveraging the unified customer view.

Capture a lead from a retailer like Lowe's or Home Depot. Automatically qualify and enrich the lead in AEP, then route it to the nearest certified installer in your network for rapid follow-up.

5. Pros & Cons of Each Approach

1. Marketo Native

  • Simplest technical setup.
  • Lower initial implementation complexity.
  • Limited to a single Dynamics instance.
  • Does not solve the core multi-CRM problem.
  • Not easily scalable for future acquisitions.

2. Marketo + Middleware

  • Connects multiple CRM instances.
  • Allows advanced data transformation.
  • High dependency on a separate tool.
  • Adds architectural complexity and cost.
  • Higher development effort for M&A.

3. AEP + AJO

  • Natively built for multi-CRM data unification.
  • Most scalable architecture for M&A.
  • Enables real-time, cross-channel journeys.
  • Represents a shift to a CDP-centric mindset.
  • AJO is a newer tool for journey orchestration.

4. AEP + Marketo (Recommended)

  • Best-of-breed: AEP for data, Marketo for B2B.
  • Natively built for multi-CRM data unification.
  • Most scalable architecture for M&A.
  • Leverages your team's potential Marketo skills.
  • Can be a larger initial technology investment.

6. Typical Project Plan & Partnership

Below reflects a high-level implementation plan that highlights to you some of the key activities and stages we would facilitate this work in a highly agile way, with an estimated range of 19-30 weeks based on the current knowns and unknowns.

Phase 1: 2-4 Weeks

Discovery and Solution Design

Our joint teams will conduct deep-dive workshops to validate requirements and audit your current data structures. The primary deliverable is a comprehensive Solution Design Document (SDD) that will serve as our blueprint for the entire project.

Phase 2: 8-12 Weeks

Implementation of New Technologies

The Shift Paradigm and Adobe teams will procure licenses and configure the core platforms based on the SDD. This phase focuses on building the foundational technology stack in a dedicated development environment.

Phase 3: 2-5 Weeks

Data Integration Between Technologies

We will build and rigorously test the data flows between your MS Dynamics instances and the new marketing platform. This ensures data integrity and that the system behaves exactly as designed before introducing marketing assets.

Phase 4: 3-5 Weeks

Migration of Critical Assets & Campaigns

Our team will strategically migrate your highest-priority assets, programs, and campaigns from Eloqua into the new platform. This includes emails, landing pages, forms, and active nurture streams to ensure business continuity.

Phase 5: 2 Weeks

Testing, UAT, and Launch

Your key users will perform User Acceptance Testing (UAT) in a sandbox environment to validate all workflows and functionality. This step is crucial for confirming the solution meets the needs of your marketing and sales teams.

Phase 6: 2 Weeks

Training, Change Management & COE Rollout

We will conduct comprehensive training sessions tailored to different user groups within your Center of Excellence and business units. Our goal is to drive strong user adoption and ensure your teams are confident from day one.

Phase 7

Monitoring, Optimizations, and Hypercare

Post-launch, we will enter a hypercare period to closely monitor system performance and resolve any issues. We then transition into a partnership focused on continuous optimization and maximizing your return on investment.
