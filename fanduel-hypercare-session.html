Supporting the Delorean Efforts

We are a dual-track partner, providing both strategic and delivery support to ensure a smooth transition and successful platform launch:

1. Strategic Thought Partner

Guiding technical and channel decisions across XP / Hightouch and how existing systems (Segment, Braze, Knime) can effectively transition to new solutions accurately and compliantly.

2. Implementation Co-Delivery

We are co-delivering the implementation of XP with the XP team, while also enabling the current Braze CRM Ops team to remain focused and dedicated to day-to-day campaign execution.

This is absolutely critical. It's important that we support and augment the current CRM Ops team to avoid any miss in active deployments while we are actively working through this technology transformation. This also includes ensuring that the migration to these new systems are "feature parity" and beyond. So there is no slip or downgrade in capability or future once cut overs do happen, and they happen gradually and intentionally, with the user/player/customer never knowing a thing about the move.

Workshop Facilitation

Our team has been key resources, helping Patty, Kunal and team facilitate key discussion points across this digital transformation.

This includes co-facilitating the Edinburgh Workshop in September in addition to the Ways of Working workshop in New Jersey in October.

This will likely result in several additional hands on sessions in the future, inclusive of the AI Workshop we will be attending in the coming weeks.

Key Items In Flight

Our focus is on foundational planning and execution to ensure successful platform integration and future growth. We have aligned on topics like:

Roadmap Alignment

Defining the phased approach for platform roll-out.

Blueprints

Think through people, process, and platforms and define optimizations in the future stack.

Audit

There are 1000s of active campaigns and assets within Braze today, we are auditing and defining what should and what shouldn't migrate over into XP. This includes template optimizations, content blocks for regulatory reasons, data, campaigns and more.

Use Case Documentation

Highlight how key capabilities must be used in the new system for example, highlighting how Push Notifications should be evolved and used in the future.

Integration Strategy

Support key seamless data flow for crucial use cases, including generosity and non-generosity campaigns and non-campaign related efforts. This includes guiding and recommending flows of data from Databricks <> High Touch <> XP and other scenarios. This also includes integration of 3rd party systems for personalization and dynamic content like Moveable Ink and the future DAM.

Iterative Channel Standup, Starting with Email Channel

Starting with email channel we're working with the XP team to clearly align and setup a strong email foundation consolidating the current 10 workspaces in braze into 1 centralized one in XP with a warmed IP and configured setup for strong email performance, then moving onto future channels like Push, In-App, Live Activity from there.

Compliance Design

Ensuring that the preference management is aligned with regulatory needs and able to be used in XP while data should be stored back into core databases.

Delivery Framework

Implementing a structure for how the right type of work gets delivered efficiently.
