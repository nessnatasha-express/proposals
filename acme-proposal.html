Proposal Strategy

Based on your team's needs, we've developed a clear path to address your immediate and long-term goals while staying within your budget guardrails and respecting your Q4 code freeze.

Value: Establishes a real-time, unified data foundation for robust cross-channel analytics and future-proofs your personalization efforts, providing an immediate, decisive replacement for Google Analytics.

Budget: Aims to fit within the all-in ceiling of ≤ $400K (licensing + services).

Timeline: Contract signature targeted for November, enabling freeze-friendly prep work in Q4 (data modeling, identity design), followed by a full January start.

  • Hypercare: This does not include post-launch hypercare or dedicated support due to expressed budget. Should you want additional post-launch support we are more than happy to support and modify.
  • Client Project Management: Assumes client provides a project manager to coordinate on your side, who will partner with the project manager on our side.
  • Managed Services: Ongoing support, campaign management, or platform administration is not included.
  • Migration: This scope is for a net-new implementation and does not include any data migration activities.
  • Complex Data Transformation: Assumes data is relatively clean. Significant ETL or data cleansing is out of scope.
  • Integrations: This estimate only includes native Adobe integrations.
  • Multiple Brands/Websites: Scope is for a single brand and single website implementation.
  • Creative Asset Development: Design of banners or content for personalization is not included.
